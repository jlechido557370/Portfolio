My Autobiography

Name: Jan Michael R. Lechido

Introduction

Hello! My name is Jan Michael R. Lechido. I was born on September 28, 2005, in Davao City, Philippines. This autobiography shares my life story, personal experiences, and aspirations. I believe that every experience, whether good or challenging, plays an important role in shaping who we become.

Early Life

I grew up in Davao City, where I spent most of my childhood exploring my interests and discovering my talents. As a child, I enjoyed playing video games, drawing, and learning new things through technology. These early experiences helped develop my creativity, curiosity, and problem-solving skills.

Education

I am currently studying at the University of Mindanao as a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) undergraduate. My studies allow me to expand my knowledge in programming, web development, and computer systems. Education plays a vital role in improving my technical skills and preparing me for my future career in the IT industry.

Hobbies & Interests

In my free time, I enjoy playing video games such as Dota 2, which helps me develop strategic thinking and teamwork skills. I also create art projects for income, combining creativity with entrepreneurship. Through these activities, I am able to relax, improve my skills, and earn extra money to support my personal goals and interests.

Goals & Dreams

My main goal in life is to complete my education and build a successful career in the field of Information Technology. I aspire to continuously improve my skills, achieve financial stability, and make a positive impact on my family and community. I believe that with dedication, hard work, and perseverance, I can turn my dreams into reality.
