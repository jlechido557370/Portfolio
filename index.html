About IVO.

IVO. is a personal brand and digital group that represents my identity and online presence across different platforms. It started as a small Discord server that I created on September 17, 2022, originally intended as a space for my friends and me to connect and have fun.

As the community gradually grew, I began to see its potential as more than just a casual server. On October 7, 2022, I designed the official IVO. logo and started developing the server with a more structured and organized approach. Since then, I have continued refining its design, structure, and overall branding to give it a more professional identity.

Although the Discord server is currently less active, it remains open to anyone who wishes to join and be part of the IVO. community. In the future, I plan to officially establish and expand IVO. as a recognized brand, with long-term goals and projects that reflect creativity, innovation, and growth.
